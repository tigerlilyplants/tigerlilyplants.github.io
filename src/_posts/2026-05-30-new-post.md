<h3>Shop announcement</h3>

Hello everyone, I wanted to take a moment to share an update and say thank you for your patience and understanding over the past few weeks.

A few weeks ago, my wife needed an unexpected surgery. Because of that, I had to make the difficult decision to cancel the open orders in my queue and temporarily place my shop in vacation mode.

I am truly sorry for the sudden change, especially to customers who were waiting on an order. I know it can be frustrating when something you were looking forward to is cancelled, and I appreciate your understanding.

At the time, my focus needed to be on my family and making sure my wife had the care and support she needed. Because everything happened quickly, I was not able to communicate as clearly or as soon as I would have liked. I apologize for that as well.

The shop is now open again, and I am grateful to be back. I am easing in carefully so I can give each order the time, attention, and care it deserves.

Thank you again for your patience, understanding, and support. It is sincerely appreciated.
